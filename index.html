Did you know?
  • Coding bootcamps/workshops are short-term programs designed to teach programming and web-development skills
  • They last between 8-24 weeks, with full-time and part-time options
  • Coding workshops often focus on specific tech stacks, such as web development, data science, or mobile app development
  • Coding workshops have gained popularity as an alternative to traditional science degrees for those looking to enter the tech industry more quickly
  • They are not guarenteed path to a job, and success depends on the effort and commitment of the individual student
  • Online coding workshops/bootcamps have become more prevalent, allowing students to learn remotely from anywhere
